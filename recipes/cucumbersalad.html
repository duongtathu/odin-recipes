Cucumber Salad

Description

This simple recipe only requires a few ingredients that you may already have on hand, and it is quick to make for a filling breakfast, lunch, or snack!

For anyone who doesn't like dill like I do, you can omit it. In addition, I usually add some sour cream and/or cream cheese to make it creamier. You can also add a can of drained tuna or salmon or boiled eggs to add some more proteins!

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber (better with English cucumber)
  • 1/4 - 1/2 onion
  • 1-3 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • ~1-2 tsp lemon juice
  • dill
  • a pinch of salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Chop cucumber into slices or small cubes.
  2. Chop onion into slices or cubes.
  3. In a large bowl, add the chopped cucumber, onion and mix in dill, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
