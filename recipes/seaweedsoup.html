Seaweed Soup

Description

This page demonstrates how to make a simple seaweed soup. This is how I and my family usually make in Vietnam.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pack Dried seaweed
  • Ground pork (optional, at least a small handfull)
  • Dried shrimp (a small handfull)
  • Green onions, optional
  • 1/4 Onions or 1 shallot and 3 cloves of garlic, minced
    • >
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Sometimes you should wash the dried seaweed, but I usually skip this step, lol
  2. Soak dried shrimp in warm water for at least 10 minutes, discard the water when use
  3. Season ground pork with cracked pepper, green onion heads, MSG, and a little bit of salt
  4. Heat a pot on medium heat, then add neutral cooking oil
  5. When the oil is hot, add minced garlic and onions, stir fry until aromatic
  6. Add the ground pork and soaked dried shrimp, cook until the pork is almost done
  7. Add water, as much as you like, some sugar, and turn the heat to high and cook until the water boils
  8. Add seaweed
  9. Turn off the heat when the water boils again
  10. Add a little bit of fish sauce, adjust seasonings to taste
  11. Top it off with some green onions, cilantros and cracked pepper
