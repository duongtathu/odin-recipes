Da Ua Da (Iced Yogurt Drink)

Description

This recipe demonstrates the steps and ingredients of a basic yogurt drink. The recipe is highly customizable! You can mix in fruits, fruit juices, syrups, club soda and more to customize it, the possibilities are endless!

My dad made me different kinds of drinks or desserts for me and my brother in the evening when we were studying in our shared room. One of the common drinks was this yogurt drink. He usually add strawberry pieces or mix coconut pulps in it. If I were at home right now, I bet he would still make me, my brother, and my mom drinks every day.

Ingredients

Again, this recipe is highly customizable, so adjust to your taste. You can choose your yogurt, plain, Greek, strawberry flavored, sweetened, etc, just remember to adjust the sugar level if you choose sweet yogurts (even consider adding less condensed milk), Same with milk, you can add strawberry milk, plain milk, or whatever. The lemon/lime juice is there to add more acidity, you can try mixing with other citrus juices... I haven't try sweet juices like watermelon juice though.

  • Plain Yogurt
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Lemon or lime juice

Steps

  1. Add about 1/4 cup of yogurt into a glass
  2. Mix in condensed milk and sugar to taste
  3. Mix in 1-3 tbsp of lemon or lime juice
  4. Add milk and/or water to a conistency that you like, I like it thin, but if you like it thick. then don't add too much liquid
  5. Mix well and enjoy with crushed ice
